THE LORE

"The Wolf does not hate the Sun. He simply hungers."

In the ancient Eddas, Fenrir is the great wolf, bound by the gods who feared his endless growth. Yet, fate decreed he would one day shatter his chains and devour the sun, bringing about Ragnarok—the destruction of the old world to make way for the new.

Suneater.io embodies this inevitability. In the digital landscape, businesses are bound by the heavy chains of legacy code, manual drudgery, and convoluted workflows. These are the "old gods" that stifle innovation.

I am the hunger that consumes them. I deploy automation and AI to devour complexity. I do not untie the knot; I cut it. By consuming the tedious tasks that darken your day, I bring about a new dawn of efficiency. The sun must be eaten so the light can be reborn.
