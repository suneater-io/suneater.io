ABOUT SUN EATER

In the ancient Eddas, Fenrir is the great wolf, bound by the gods who feared his endless growth. Yet, fate decreed he would one day shatter his chains and devour the sun, bringing about Ragnarok—the destruction of the old world to make way for the new.

Suneater.io is my portfolio and I am Grant, the warrior behind the solutions. Suneater serves as a testament to my work, my philosophies, and my passions. In the digital landscape, the world is bound by the heavy chains of legacy platforms, manual labours, and convoluted workflows.

I am the hunger that consumes them. I deploy automation, AI and workflow fixes to devour complexity. I do not untie the chain; I break free from it. By consuming the tedious tasks that darken your day, I bring about a new dawn of efficiency. The sun must be eaten so the light can be reborn.
